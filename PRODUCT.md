# PRODUCT

Register: product

## Product

Bloomjoy Hub is the operating system for Bloomjoy's robotic cotton candy business. It supports public buyers, machine buyers, operators, Machine Managers, admins, and internal operations across marketing, quote intake, supplies, training, refunds, reporting, and partner workflows.

## Primary Audiences

- Public visitors evaluating Bloomjoy products, memberships, supplies, and event or procurement paths.
- Operators and Machine Managers who need fast access to machines, training, refund status, and operational tasks.
- Admins who manage machines, refunds, reporting, access, orders, and launch operations.
- Partners and internal stakeholders who need reliable exports, scheduled reporting, and clear operational evidence.

## Product Principles

- Operational clarity beats decoration. Admin and operator surfaces should be quiet, dense enough for repeated work, and easy to scan.
- The app should make the right next action obvious without long explanatory text.
- Public pages can feel warm, premium, and candy-forward, but still need to be concrete about the offer and path to action.
- Sensitive flows such as refunds, reporting, access, payments, and vendor syncs must prioritize trust, reviewability, and rollback.
- GitHub Issues and the Bloomjoy Project board are the live source of truth for active work. Static docs hold durable context only.

## Experience Notes

- Default register is `product` for app, portal, admin, reporting, refunds, training, and operator surfaces.
- Public marketing pages may use a warmer `brand` register when the task explicitly targets buyer-facing storytelling.
- Avoid generic SaaS dashboards, fake metrics, vague candy whimsy, and decorative complexity that slows operational users down.
- Never expose secrets, raw customer data, payment IDs, vendor exports, or free-text complaint content in UI, docs, issues, PRs, or chat.
